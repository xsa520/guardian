## Guardian's Structural Position

Guardian does not compete with any of the above layers.

Guardian addresses the structural question that emerges
when any two of the above layers must coordinate:

"Whether decisions produced under separately governed
systems remain formally comparable, reconcilable, and
acceptance-compatible across governance boundaries."
