# Guardian — Declarative to Equivalence Corridor

## Concept

Thomas Gessler's Declarative AI framework establishes
how governance becomes system logic within a governed
environment.

Guardian's decision equivalence layer addresses what
happens when independently governed declarative systems
must coordinate across boundaries.

## Stack

Layer 1 — Declarative Governance (Thomas Gessler)
Rules, constraints, authority scopes become explicit,
versioned, inspectable knowledge artifacts.

Layer 2 — Deterministic Execution (Kamilla / IFA)
Admissibility computed at runtime. Refusal structural.
Authority centralized. AI advises, never authorizes.

Layer 3 — Decision Equivalence (Guardian)
Whether decisions produced under separately governed
declarative systems remain formally comparable,
reconcilable, and acceptance-compatible across
governance boundaries.
